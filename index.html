Mountain Rescue
I like hiking, especially in Lake District. I am grateful I never had any accident although there were some nerve-wrecking moment when I accidentally went the wrong way. Nonetheless, there are times some walkers need help, and the amazing mountain rescuers come to their rescue. So I have made a visualisation to illustrate their hardwork.
